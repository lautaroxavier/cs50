Characters

Violetta Valery

Violetta Valéry is a beautiful and popular courtesan in Paris, who suffers from tuberculosis. She falls in love with Alfredo, a young nobleman, and decides to leave her life of luxury for him.

Alfredo Germont

Alfredo Germont is a passionate and romantic young man, who is captivated by Violetta’s charm and beauty.

Giorgio Germont

Giorgio Germont is a stern and conservative father, who disapproves of his son’s affair with a courtesan.
